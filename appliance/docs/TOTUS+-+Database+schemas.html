NIWA Systems : TOTUS - Database schemas

TOTUS

TOTUS is a collection of interrelated data schemas. TOTUS integrates census, traffic congestion and air quality information with a Open Street Map street network and a routing engine.

  • Census
  • TrafficModel traffic model
  • Energy
  • Exposure
  • Open Street Map
  • Routing network
